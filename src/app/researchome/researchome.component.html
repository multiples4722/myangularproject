Research Objectives and Overall Strategy

Secure and Safe Assured Autonomy will address the following four Technical Challenges (TCs) and will provide unique Urban Air Mobility (UAM) solutions and a transition roadmap for industry/government to utilize resulting products

  • TC1:Safe Perception, Coordination, Planning, and Navigation
  • TC2:Secured Autonomy
  • TC3:Verification & Validation (V&V) and Test & Evaluation (T&E)
  • TC4:System Integration
